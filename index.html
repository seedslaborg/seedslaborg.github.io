Home

SEEDS research group works on every kind of technology development related to data science. More concretely, by looking at the current real-world problems, we research on data and other related areas to find out the solution.

“SEEDS” means “SoftwarE IntElligence and Data Science”. The main research interest of SEEDS includes data mining, machine learning, semantic web, software security, artificial intelligence, and some other related areas. Currently our research mainly involves: ensemble learning, semi-supervised learning, class-imbalance learning, association rule mining, software code analysis, software fault localization, etc.
